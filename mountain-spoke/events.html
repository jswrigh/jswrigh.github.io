Ride the Divide

It's not too early to sign up and get ready for our annual overnight "Ride the Divide" event. This is the biggest event of the year. Some of our customers and friends (is there a difference?) have been with us on every ride since the first one in 2009. This ride is not for beginners. If you aren't sure whether you are up to the challenge, stop by the store or drop us a note.


It you're still reading this, we haven't scared you away. Good for you! Your next step is to register soon so you can be one of the 35 rugged cyclists to join us. Here are the details:

  • Depart at 3:00pm Friday, 7 September from Targhee Pass
  • Return by 6:00pm on Saturday, 8 September
  • Ride 30 miles Friday and 50 on Saturday
  • Camp at Targhee Peak
We'll send you a packet full of information when you register.
